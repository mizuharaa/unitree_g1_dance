  35 Side Flip samples -- these two are combo clips:
  449: 侧空翻转体360°+旋子转体360°+跌竖叉 "side flip w/ 360 twist + butterfly twist (xuanzi)
       360 + drop into split" - Scene-004.
  464: 侧空翻转体360°和旋风脚360°+马步 "side flip 360 & whirlwind kick 360 + horse stance"
       - Scene-005.

FORMAT: identical to backflip candidate (fps=30, qpos (T,36), root quat WXYZ, 29 joints in
  our exact order). Same trivial conversion to our CSV.

DURATION / EVIDENCE (computed)
  449: 163 frames @30fps = 5.43 s; roll-plane sweep 415 deg with 2 fully-inverted frames,
       peak root z 0.75 m -> genuine aerial side rotation. BETTER of the two.
  464: 147 frames @30fps = 4.90 s; pitch sweep 139 / roll sweep 127 deg, NO fully-inverted
       frames -> the retarget likely attenuated the flip; treat as a dynamic jump-spin-kick
       reference rather than a true flip.
